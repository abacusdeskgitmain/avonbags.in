  ABOUT US  
 

In recent times demand for world class bags has been rising from various market segment especially the teenagers. Responding to the very strong suggestions from across the network, Avon Cycles embarked upon a standardization program to support and strengthen their product line.

Futuristik, an associate of Avon Cycles Limited is now fully engaged manufacturing and marketing of Avon Branded bags. The product range will include various kinds of bags like school, travel and totes, soft luggage, kits, backpacks, executive etc. And Yes the best part, Avon branded bags will offer excellent quality at surprisingly low prices which will come with the Avon guarantee.

Our state of art production unit in Faridabad will ensure the quality and new innovation in the product.

Avon branded bags will be presented with the same zeal, commitment and innovation that has been done in the past half century of Avon Cycles Limited.
